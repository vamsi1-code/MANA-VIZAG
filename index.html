City Of Destiny

Introduction

Visakhapatnam or Vizag is considered to be a port city of the state of Andhra Pradesh. The people living in this city belong to different religions, cultures, and race live with love and harmony. This city is concealed between the hills of the Eastern Ghats facing towards the direction of Bay of Bengal. It is also referred to as the “city of destiny” and “the jewel city of the east coast.”







How I Can Reach VIZAG?

Visakhapatnam is well-connected by air, rail, and road. It has its own airport, Visakhapatnam Airport (VTZ), with regular flights from major cities in India. The city also has a major railway station, Visakhapatnam Junction, which is connected to various cities across the country by regular trains. Additionally, there are well-maintained highways connecting Visakhapatnam to nearby cities and states.



Airport

The passenger terminal can handle 700 arriving and 1300 departing passengers. It covers an area of 31,400 square meters. The airport has a total of 18 parking bays



RTC Complex

APSRTC has bus services traversing the length and breadth of this city as residents of Visakhapatnam prefer city buses that extent across the city and its suburbs. .



Railway Station

Visakhapatnam is one of the major railway stations in the south central zone of Indian railways, situated in Andhra Pradesh .The Visakhapatnam railway station lies on main Chennai Howrah route.

Vizag Tourism

Some popular tourist attractions in Visakhapatnam include RK Beach, Kailasagiri Hill Park, Araku Valley, Yarada Beach, Bheemli, Rushikonda, Dolphin’s Nose, Submarine Museum, and Simhachalam Temple,.
